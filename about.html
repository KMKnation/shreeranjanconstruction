About The Firm

From infrastructure roots to a broader construction identity.

The company story begins with Somlalit Contractors, a contracting firm operating since 2006 in infrastructure projects. In 2022, the business expanded under Shree Ranjan Construction to serve residential, commercial, and wider civil construction requirements.

Company Overview

A business shaped by quality, technical expertise, and long-term client trust.

The company profile centers on professional construction delivery, management strength, and the ability to serve both structural building work and large-scale infrastructure requirements.

2006 Somlalit Contractors begins infrastructure contracting operations.
2022 Shree Ranjan Construction expands the business into broader civil and construction execution.
Today The firm serves residential, commercial, industrial, and infrastructure projects with a premium corporate presence.
