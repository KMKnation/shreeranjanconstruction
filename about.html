About The Firm

From infrastructure roots to a broader construction identity.

The company story begins with Somlalit Contractors, a contracting firm operating since 2006 in infrastructure projects. In 2022, the business expanded under Shree Ranjan Construction to serve residential, commercial, and wider civil construction requirements.

Company Overview

A reputation built in the field, then refined into a broader construction identity.

Shree Ranjan Construction represents the next chapter of an operating legacy that began in infrastructure contracting. The firm combines execution discipline, technical confidence, and a corporate-facing presentation suited to modern civil, industrial, and building work.

Legacy Foundation Somlalit Contractors has been active since 2006, establishing the company’s reputation in infrastructure and civil execution.
Strategic Expansion In 2022, Shree Ranjan Construction expanded the business into a wider construction offering across residential, commercial, and industrial works.
Execution Profile The company supports projects that demand site coordination, technical understanding, quality control, and dependable milestone delivery.
Brand Presence Today, the firm positions itself as an engineering-led contractor with heritage credibility and a premium, client-facing identity.
